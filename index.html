about

As I grew up in the 90s as part of our rich and awesome rising Techno and Club Culture in Europe. In those Days I was clubbing in Munich in the old Kunstpark. First at the old Airport in Munich and then at the old Kunstpark Ost at the East Train Station in Munich. There I was clubbing at Ultaschall wehre Monika Kruse was at that time the resident DJ for the Uschall and I witnessed great Partys and Events in the old Babylon and great Goa Events in the Natraj Temple with there Legendary Chill Out Area up stairs. In those Days my addiction to Techno the music and Club scene got shaped, those where the good old days of my youth, where it all begun as specialy with my favor for Acid Techno and the sound of the good old Roland TB-303.
